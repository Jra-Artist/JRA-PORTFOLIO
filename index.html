About

Hi, I'm John Robert L. Arevalo, a 20-year-old creative professional studying at Cavite State University - Bacoor. My journey into digital art began when I first saw the creation of moving drawings, inspiring me to explore digital art and web design to create appealing and functional designs for clients.

My expertise spans traditional and digital drawing, as well as web design. Influential artists such as Stan Lee, Akira Toriyama, Tite Kubo, and Eiichiro Oda have shaped my artistic style.

I'm inspired by innovative web design, striving for continuous improvement. I'm open to feedback and eager to collaborate on web design or digital art projects.
